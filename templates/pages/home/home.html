(Required component)
In this module, you will be participating in an interview in which our interviewer agent will ask you about yourself and your experiences with work.

IMPORTANT:
Ensure the followings before starting the interview.

  • Please find a quiet place where you can talk comfortably during this interview. Background noise can make it difficult to record and pick up your voice, hindering the interview process.
  • Your computer will need a microphone, like the one you might have used for online calls (e.g., Zoom, Google Hangouts). If you are an AirPod user, we recommend against using its microphone for this interview.
  • Please only use one of the following browsers: Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Opera, Edge
Please note (if the interview is stalling):
During the interview, three dots (loading sign) should only last on average 5 seconds, and maximum 2 minutes, and will proceed to the next question without you having to do anything. If the interviewer is not responding/stalling for longer than a minute, please refresh the page. Finally, we strongly recommend that you use a desktop/laptop for this part of the study (instead of a mobile/tablet device).
